About

The short version

I'm a software engineer currently building GTM AI tools and workflows. My background is a mix of marketing, business, and engineering, which gives me a practical lens on software: I care not only about how systems are built, but whether they solve real problems, change behavior, and create leverage.

I'm a first-generation Asian American and the daughter of immigrants. I grew up with parents who taught me to work hard, be resourceful, and take opportunities seriously. That has shaped how I move through my career, and made me interested in how people build momentum without always having a clear roadmap.

Lately I've been interested in how AI can help people turn messy, repeated work into reliable, repeatable workflows.
